Biome Travel Destinations

Mediterranean Forest (Spain, Portugal & Italy)

Warm coastal forests across Spain, Portugal, and Italy with mild winters and nice summers.

A Mediterranean Forest also exsists in places like Chili, Califorina, and South Africa.

Price: $2225

Duration: 6 Days

Temperate Forest (PA, Ohio, WV)

Experience forests, mountains, the Ohio River, and Lake Erie.

This enviorment can also be seen in places like France, Germany, China, and Japan.

Price: $1400

Duration: 4 Days

Tropical Rainforest (Southern Mexico)

In Southern Mexico you can see lush jungles as well and nice beaches and warm weather.

Tropical rainforests can also be seen in places like South America, Southeast Asia and Central Africa.

Price: $1900

Duration: 5 Days

Desert (Morocco)

In the Morrocan Desert you can explore vast sand dunes, hot days, and cool nights.

Deserts can also be found in places like the Southwestern United States and in Northern Mexico.

Price: $2442

Duration: 5 Days

Taiga (Norway)

Visit mountainous regions with dense taiga forests and experience the Northern Lights.

This can be seen in places like Northern Canada and Alaska.

Price: $2000

Duration: 7 Days
